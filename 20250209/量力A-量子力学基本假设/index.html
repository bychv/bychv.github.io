Fondemental Postulates (Axions) of Quantum Mechanics

State Postulates

state vector $\ket{\psi} \in \mathcal{H}$, $\bra{\psi}\in \mathcal{H}^*$, $\mathcal{H}$ is Hilbert space.
$(\bra{\psi})^\dagger = \ket{\psi}$, $(\ket{\psi})^\dagger = \bra{\psi}$. $\dagger$ is adjoint/Hermitian conjugate.$\dagger$: Transpose + Complex conjugate.

Oprator Postulates

Physical quantities $\leftrightarrow$ Obeservables $\leftrightarrow$ Operator $\hat{A}$(linear Hermitian).

Measurement Postulates

  1. Measurement Outcome: $\hat{A}\ket{u_n}=A_n\ket{u_n}$.$A_n$ is eigenvalue, $\ket{u_n}$ is eigenstate $.
  2. pabability of $A_n$ in $\ket{\psi}$: $P(A_n)=|\braket{u_n|\psi}|^2$. $\ket{\psi}=\sum_n c_n\ket{u_n}$, $c_n=\braket{u_n|\psi}$.
  3. State collapse: After measurement, the state is $\ket{u_n}$.

Dynamics Postulates

Schrodinger Equation: $\i\hbar\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\ket{\psi}=\hat{H}\ket{\psi}$

Identical Particles Postulates

State Vector

Vector additon

$ \ket{\psi_1}+\ket{\psi_2}=\ket{\psi_2}+\ket{\psi_1} \in \mathcal{H}$

Scalar multiplication

$a(\ket{\psi_1}+\ket{\psi_2})=a\ket{\psi_1}+a\ket{\psi_2}$
$(a+b)\ket{\psi}=a\ket{\psi}+b\ket{\psi}$
$a,b\in \mathbb{C}$

Inner product

$\braket{\psi|\phi}=c \in \mathbb{C}$
$\braket{\psi|\phi}=\braket{\phi|\psi}^*$
$\braket{\psi|\psi}=r \in \mathbb{R} \geq 0 ,\text{especially iff} \ket{\psi}=0$

Dyadic product

$(\ket{\psi}\bra{\phi})^\dagger = \ket{\phi}\bra{\psi}$

Operator

$\hat{A}\ket{\psi}=\ket{\phi}$
Two kinds of operators in QM.

  1. Obeservable: linear, hermitian
    $\hat{H}^\dagger=\hat{H}$
  2. Transformation: Restrict to linear and unitary transformation
    $\hat{U}^\dagger\hat{U}=\hat{U}\hat{U}^\dagger=\mathbb{1}$
    Linearity: $\hat{L}(a\ket{\psi}+b\ket{\phi})=a\hat{L}\ket{\psi}+b\hat{L}\ket{\phi}$

Adjoint

$\forall \ket{\psi}, \hat{A}\ket{\psi}=\hat{B}\ket{\psi} \Rightarrow \hat{A}=\hat{B}$
$(\hat{A}\hat{B})\ket{\psi}=\hat{A}(\hat{B}\ket{\psi})$
Define Commutator $[\hat{A},\hat{B}]=\hat{A}\hat{B}-\hat{B}\hat{A}$

Properties of Commutator

$[\hat{A},\hat{B}]= - [\hat{B},\hat{A}]$
$[\hat{A},hat{B+C}]=[\hat{A},\hat{B}]+[\hat{A},\hat{C}]$
$[a\hat{A},b\hat{B}+c\hat{C}]=ab[\hat{A},\hat{B}]+ac[\hat{A},\hat{C}]$
$[\hat{A},\hat{B}\hat{C}]=[\hat{A},\hat{B}]\hat{C}+\hat{B}[\hat{A},\hat{C}]$
$[\hat{A}\hat{B},\hat{C}]=[\hat{A},\hat{C}]\hat{B}+\hat{A}[\hat{B},\hat{C}]$
$[\hat{A},\hat{B}^n]=n[\hat{A},\hat{B}]\hat{B}^{n-1},\big([\hat{A},[\hat{A},\hat{B}]]=0\big)$
$[\hat{A},f(\hat{B})]=[\hat{A},\hat{B}]\frac{\partial f}{\partial \hat{B}}$
$[\hat{A},[\hat{B},\hat{C}]+[\hat{C},[\hat{A},\hat{B}]+[\hat{B},[\hat{C},\hat{A}]]=0$

Adjoint (Hermitian Conjugate)

$$\big(\hat{A}\ket{\psi}\big)^\dagger = \bra{\psi}\hat{A}^\dagger $$

$$\bra{\psi}\hat{A}^\dagger\ket{\phi} = \braket{\phi|\hat{A}|\psi}^* $$
